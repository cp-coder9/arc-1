Escrow workflow across the project

Approval-gated
1. Contract / appointmentPayment schedule, milestones and release rules are created from contract or appointment.
2. Responsible party paysClient, BEP or contractor uses the payment gateway depending on who must pay the invoice.
3. Work completedBEP, freelancer, contractor or subcontractor submits deliverable, claim or invoice.
4. Evidence attachedDrawings, reports, photos, QS measurement, delivery notes or approval records are linked.
5. Approval gateClient, BEP, QS, principal agent, contractor or admin approves based on workflow role.
6. Release / holdFunds are released, partially released, retained or held if disputed.
7. Audit trailEvery decision, AI check, invoice and payment release is logged.
